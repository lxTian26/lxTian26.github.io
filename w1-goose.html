The Goose Mountain

The website I created is basically a promotion for a short film named The Goose Mountain. And try to immerse the user in this amazing story through only CSS and HTML by moving and clicking the cursor.


Can experience by Clicking Here (GitHub)


The website got 78 and 85 marks for IA and Visual Design and Layout design respectively with 80 marks for the use of css language in assessment.

Why This?

The website attempts to share subjective analysis and ideas about the short film by adding paragraphs and text as well as relevant images at the same time, therefore making it easier for people who are unaware of this short film to notice what this film may be trying to display and thus generate their own perspectives about this film.

Moreover, I want to bring an immersive and interactive storytelling experience through images and texts, and enable viewr feel the unique atmosphere of Chinese ink paintings.

How this Web is structured?



Web IA Design

To smoothly present the whole story, the order of links on the site was carefully considered.

Design of the web structure according to web develop principle (e.g. principle of front doors, principle of growth...)

2023 Spring

Layout Design (CSS)

The page design was especially important due to the fact that I wanted this site to show the feel of a comic strip. As a result, typography took up a great amount of the work. By grouping and arranging the text and images, fixing their position and dynamics, a better interactive experience is achieved.

The accessibility such as different devices are also considered when developing user Interface.

The scrolling method (horizontal) breaks from the usual web usage and allows the user to discover the web experience independently by controlling the position of the text and adding appropriate hints. The swiping of sentences and paragraphs is also used to simulate the feeling of storytelling.

Reflection

As the website is more experiential and storytelling oriented, if the user has generated a personal understanding of the story and(or) a deeper reflection after visiting the website, it will be a successful promotion of the film in my personal opinion. Similarly, if users are interested in watching the original film after experiencing the website through the aesthetic elements and characters, then this can also be considered as an effective advertisement.

Improvements

Although the site is compatible with the three screen sizes in the request, it cannot be guaranteed to perfectly match the remaining screen sizes.

For Designing, perhaps some sound effects should be added when the user clicks or touches an object, and some background music as well. And the experience can be enriched by adding some java scripts in the future, such as scrolling to display text and images.

For Coding, as this is the first time I have worked on a website and I was not familiar with it when I first started, the class name was created in a rather messy and difficult to read manner.
