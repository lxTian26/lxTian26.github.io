- Film Details -

This short film Goose Mountain is from the second episode of Yao-Chinese Folktales released by Shanghai Animation Film Studio, and is based on the story Yang Xian Scholar written by Jun Wu of the Liang Dynasty.

- About this Story -

This short film focuses on the bizarre story of what a pedilar who enters the Goose Mountain to transport two geese witnesses and experiences after accidentally encountering a fox scholar. Through the use of traditional ink textures, unique character styles and spooky background music, it successfully portrays a dream-like story with a strong Chinese aesthetic of absurdity and grotesqueness.
