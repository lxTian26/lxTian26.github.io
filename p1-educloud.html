Back Web Design - EduCloud

(Group Project)


An online learning platform designed for university students and teachers, optimising the existing VLE platform to support a simpler, faster way of operating.

During this group collaboration, I was mainly responsible for time management and planning, pre-research and creation of presentation slides.

Why this?

The VLE (Virtual Learning Environment) at universities in general is overloaded with features that are cumbersome and complex to use, which affects the efficiency of the learning process, thus we want to optimise it so that it is easy and straightforward to use for both students and teachers. Overall, this has been designed to create a comfortable and confusion-free experience.

How to design a confusion-free VLE?

Detailed Design

After organising the resources and information we found, our VLE is then designed. The wireframe has been considered to easily prioritise and decide the locations of each function or item, and to visualise the online teaching tool on the user interface by analysing user requirements.

Wireframes are created by using Balsamiq (Done by Teammates)

2022 Summer

Highly Functional Prototype Development

(Done by Teammates)


Based on the wireframe, a high-fidelity prototype of Educloud has been developed at the end of the design development process using Adobe XD.

In general, the concept of the prototype design focuses on the efficiency of studying and the convenience of finding what users want such as learning resources. The prototype also features a dark and light mode toggle, as decided by the responses from our user data.

Reflection


Critical Reflection

As it was the first time we working as a group, I think our group were doing our jobs well. The development from the wireframe to the prototype worked especially well, as the high-fidelity prototype reflects and builds upon the ideas established by the wireframe successfully.

We were able to effectively delegate group tasks as well as manage our time well by setting deadlines for different tasks and setting goals for each meeting. We able to friendly use Instagram groups to communicate and a shared Google Drive to store files.

Although we faced some limitation when we collect userbased data and information, Educloud still solves some of the challenges of using existing online teaching tools as informed by our user data during the process of design development, as students’ frustrations concerning using online teaching tools have been well considered. Especially, the clear layout of the Educloud prototype might be able to solve students’ difficulties in finding modules and learning resources. Educators’ challenges in encouraging students to participate in modules and create a connection among students have also been considered.

Personal Reflection

My main responsibility in this group work was more on the managerial aspects, I acted as the role of listing the work arrangement in each meeting and actively guided the group in discussing our project design. In addition, I was also mainly responsible for the production and layout of the slides, as we needed to rely on the final presentation as a criterion of quality for this report.

Although the work may not seem complicated, this teamwork experience has improved my planning skills, and to a certain extent enhanced my ability to manage the progress of the team's project and communication skills.
